Account and Login

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it does not arrive.

If you no longer have access to the email you signed up with, contact us at afosi.2025@gmail.com.

Try resetting your password first. If the issue continues, make sure you are using the email address you signed up with — not a username or phone number.

If you signed up with Google or another provider, use that same login method on the login screen.

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account, posts, and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

Feed and Posts

The For You feed personalises as you use the app. When your account is new, it shows recent public posts from across the platform. Follow some users and like posts on topics you care about — the feed improves quickly after a few interactions.

Tap the three dots (...) on any of your posts and select Delete. Deleted posts are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Direct Messages

Tap the message icon in the top right of the home screen, then tap the compose button to start a new conversation. You can message anyone you follow or who follows you.

In your messages list, swipe left on the conversation and tap Delete. This removes the conversation from your view only.

AI Guide

The Kiongozi AI is your civic guide. It can answer questions about Kenya's Constitution, how county and national government works, public finance, devolution, civic rights, and the green economy.

Open it anytime by tapping the green Kiongozi button or by mentioning @kiongozi in any post or comment.

AI responses may occasionally be incomplete or out of date. For critical civic or legal matters, always verify with official sources such as kenyalaw.org or kenya.go.ke.

You can report a bad response by tapping the flag icon on any AI message, or email us at afosi.2025@gmail.com.

Safety and Reporting

Tap the three dots (...) on any post, comment, or profile and select Report. Choose the reason that best applies. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team within 24 hours.

Visit the user's profile, tap the three dots in the top right corner, and select Block. Blocked users cannot see your profile, posts, or send you messages. You can manage your block list in Settings → Privacy → Blocked Accounts.

Use the Report option on the content or profile and select Child Safety. These reports are escalated immediately. You can also email afosi.2025@gmail.com directly — we respond within 24 hours.

For more information, read our Child Safety Standards.

Civic Tools

Fund Tracker lets you follow public funds and government spending in Kenya. Data is sourced from the Kenya National Treasury (treasury.go.ke) and the Controller of Budget (cob.go.ke). Kiongozi Chat is not a government platform — this information is presented for civic education purposes.

Youth Voice is a space for Kenyan youth to submit their views on national policies and issues. Your submissions are aggregated to surface community opinions on governance matters. It is a civic participation tool, not an official government feedback channel.
