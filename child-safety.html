Child Safety Standards

Kiongozi Chat's commitments to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

About Kiongozi Chat

Kiongozi Chat (com.kiongozi.chat) is a civic social network for Kenyan youth and citizens. The platform enables users to connect with peers, track public funds, engage in civic discussions, and access an AI guide on governance, the green economy, and youth empowerment.

Kiongozi Chat requires all users to be at least 13 years of age to register. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Accounts found to belong to users under 13 are suspended and their data deleted.

Zero Tolerance for CSAE Content

Kiongozi Chat maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy for any content, behaviour, or communication involving child sexual abuse or exploitation. This includes:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form, including images, video, text, audio, or links
  • Grooming, sexual solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Trafficking or recruitment of children for sexual purposes
  • Any content that sexualises individuals under 18
  • Sharing or promoting CSAE content in posts, comments, or direct messages

Violations result in immediate and permanent account termination. Confirmed CSAE content is reported to the relevant national and regional authorities, including the Communications Authority of Kenya and, where applicable, the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC).

In-App Reporting

Users can report any child safety concern directly within the app:

  • Every post, comment, and direct message has a Report option
  • Every user profile can be reported for child safety concerns
  • Reports are reviewed within 24 hours, and CSAE-related reports are escalated immediately
  • Reporters remain anonymous to the reported party at all times

Kiongozi Chat allows users to report child safety concerns in-app. Every user has direct access to reporting tools on all content and profiles at all times.

Content Moderation and Safeguards

  • Automated screening for known CSAM signatures using hash-matching technology
  • AI-assisted detection for grooming language and inappropriate communications
  • Age verification at registration enforcing the 13+ minimum age requirement
  • Private account mode allowing users under 18 to restrict followers and messages
  • Direct message controls requiring mutual opt-in before receiving messages from unknown accounts
  • User blocking and muting tools available on every profile
  • Trained moderation staff reviewing all flagged content and accounts

Legal Compliance

Kiongozi Chat complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including:

  • The Kenya Children Act 2022 and its provisions on child online protection
  • The Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act 2018 (Kenya) governing online offences against minors
  • The Data Protection Act 2019 (Kenya) with respect to processing data of children
  • Google Play's Families Policy and developer content guidelines
  • Apple App Store's Review Guidelines on child safety

Kiongozi Chat cooperates fully with law enforcement investigations involving child safety and proactively reports confirmed CSAE to regional and national authorities as required by law.
