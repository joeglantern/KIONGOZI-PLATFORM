# Kiongozi Platform

## What is Kiongozi Platform?

Kiongozi Platform is a civic education tool designed for Kenyan youth to learn about governance, elections, and their rights as citizens. The name "Kiongozi" means "leader" in Swahili, reflecting our mission to empower the next generation of Kenyan leaders.

## Key Features

### 💬 Interactive AI Assistant
- Ask questions about Kenyan governance, elections, and civic duties
- Get immediate, accurate responses based on Kenyan law and policy
- No need to search through complex government websites

### 📚 Curated Topics
- Explore suggested topics including voting, governance, rights, and more
- Each topic comes with sample questions to get you started
- Topics are regularly updated and can be customized to match your interests

### 🧠 AI-Generated Topics
- Topics are dynamically generated by AI to ensure relevance and comprehensiveness
- IEBC education and Youth Leadership topics provide specialized knowledge about electoral processes
- Users can refresh the topic list to discover new areas of civic education
- Topic selection is personalized and saved for future sessions

### 🔍 Deep Research Mode
- Switch to research mode for more comprehensive answers
- Get cited sources and references for further reading
- Perfect for students, researchers, and citizens looking for verified information

### 🌓 User-Friendly Design
- Clean, intuitive interface that works on both computers and mobile devices
- Dark mode for comfortable reading in any environment
- Accessible design that prioritizes ease of use

### ✨ Highly Interactive Experience
- Engaging animations and transitions create a dynamic learning environment
- Interactive topic exploration with expandable categories sparks curiosity
- Real-time responses with typing effects make conversations feel natural and engaging
- Designed to encourage continuous exploration and deeper civic understanding
- Each answer leads to new questions, creating a self-sustaining learning journey

## Technology

Built using modern web technologies including Next.js and AI integration, the platform provides fast, reliable civic information for all Kenyans.

## Project Goals

1. **Increase civic awareness** among Kenyan youth
2. **Simplify complex information** about governance and elections
3. **Empower citizens** with knowledge about their rights and responsibilities
4. **Create a reliable source** of civic information free from political bias
5. **Foster continued engagement** by making civic education interactive and exciting
